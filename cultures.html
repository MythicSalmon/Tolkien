The Cultures and Races of Arda

Races include the inhabitants of Eä which are but not limited to—the Dwarves, the Elves, the Ents, the Hobbits, the Maiar, the Men, the Orcs, the Spirits, the Undead, and the Valar. The cultures of the inhabitants of Eä are the unique expression of beliefs and lifestyles they possess in and of the world around them.
